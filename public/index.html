About Us

The Energy and Environment Conservation Club is a passionate community of students and faculty committed to making a real difference for our planet. Since our founding, we have worked tirelessly to promote sustainability, environmental awareness, and responsible resource use within our college and the wider community.

Through workshops, campaigns, and hands-on projects, we empower individuals to adopt eco-friendly habits and inspire collective action. Our initiatives range from large-scale tree plantation drives and campus clean-ups to educational seminars on renewable energy and waste management. We believe that every small step counts, and together, we can create a greener, healthier future for all.
