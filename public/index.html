About Us

The Energy and Environment Conservation Club (EECC) of Kongu Engineering College, established in 2006, is a student-driven initiative aimed at fostering awareness and action in the fields of energy conservation and environmental sustainability. The club operates under the vision of creating a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable world through education, engagement, and environmental responsibility.

EECC strives to instill eco-conscious values among students by organizing various programs such as awareness rallies, workshops, seminars, energy audits, tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and competitions. These activities not only promote environmental protection but also encourage students to become responsible citizens and change-makers in society.

In collaboration with government bodies, NGOs, and industry experts, the club provides a platform for students to explore real-world environmental challenges and innovative solutions. EECC plays a pivotal role in integrating sustainability practices within the campus and beyond, aligning with national missions such as Swachh Bharat and energy efficiency goals.
