About Us

The Energy and Environment Conservation Club (EEC) at Kongu Engineering College is a student-run initiative established in 2006 dedicated to promoting sustainability and environmental awareness. Through workshops, tree plantation drives, and clean energy projects, EECC actively engages students in practical conservation efforts while collaborating with NGOs and government bodies on ecological initiatives.

Kongu Engineering College (KEC)

Kongu Engineering College, established in 1983 by the Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust, is a premier technical institution renowned for its industry-aligned education and research programs. Located in Erode, Tamil Nadu, KEC provides an interdisciplinary learning environment that balances theoretical knowledge with practical application.
